We're excited to announce that the SKEG 2026 Mini-Symposium will take place **April 13-14, 2026** as a virtual event!

## What To Expect:

Following last year's success, we're bringing back all the popular formats:
- Daily Keynote presentations
- Research presentations (15 min)
- Idea pitches (8-10 min)
- Speed date introductions

**Plus:** We're letting YOU help decide the session timing! When registration opens in mid February, your location and time zone preferences will help us schedule sessions that work for the majority of participants.

## Timeline

- **Mid February:** Registration and call for presentations open
- **March 2:** Presentation submissions due
- **Mid-March:** Full agenda published
- **April 13-14:** Symposium days!

## Can't Attend Live?

No problem! Recordings, slides, and discussion opportunities will be available for all participants.

Stay tuned for more details in the coming weeks!
